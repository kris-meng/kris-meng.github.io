// 01 — about

Hi! I'm Kristal

I'm an ML researcher working at the intersection of neuroscience and healthcare. I completed my Master's in Computer Science at York University, where I built transformer-based models for time-series classification and forecasting. Before that, I studied neuroscience at the University of Toronto and worked on clinical research at Toronto Western Hospital, using neuroimaging (CT/MRI), iEEG, and physiological data.

My work focuses on translating research into practical systems, particularly for applications like brain stimulation (TMS, DBS) and clinical decision tools. More recently, I've been exploring LLMs and agentic AI for biomedical and healthcare use cases.

Outside of work, I enjoy playing volleyball and painting.
